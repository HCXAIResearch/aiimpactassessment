# 1. How to Conduct an AI Impact Assessment

**AI Impact Assessment:** this is a procedure employed by entities (businesses or institutions etc.) to assess how an AI solution (app, system or model etc.) might affect its users, primary (intended) and extended (beneficiary/stakeholder) and potentially the environment. The scope of the depth in carrying out the evaluation depends on the entity's goals and resources. It targets finding out both the positives and negative attributes, the risks and benefits. If done correctly, the assessment should help identify potential issues with the solution early, help with legal and ethical compliance, and inherently it would build trust in its users.

## What’s the ISO/IEC 42005:2025?

**ISO/IEC 42005:2025** is a standard that provides industry guidance for  enterprises to perform AI impact assessments, with major focus on how  these systems could pose a risk or give benefits to individuals and society. The standard recommends that **AI impact assessments should be  integrated into existing AI risk management and organizational  processes**, rather than treating them as standalone tasks (Chibueze, 2025).

### Process outline

1. Define scope and context

2. Integarte to existing processes

3. Establish timing and trigger events

4. Assign roles and responsibilities

5. Conduct the assessment

6. Document and share findings

7. Implemenet monitoring, review and remedial mechanisms
